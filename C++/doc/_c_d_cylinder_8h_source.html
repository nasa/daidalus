17  *
18  * REMARK: X points to East, Y points to North.
19  *
20  * Naming Convention
21  * -----------------
22  * The intruder is fixed at the origin of the coordinate system.
23  *
24  * D : Diameter of the protected zone [d]
25  * H : Height of the protected zone [d]
26  * B : Lower bound of lookahed time interval [t] (B >= 0)
27  * T : Upper bound of lookahead time interval [t] (T < 0 means infinite lookahead time)
28  * s : Relative 3-D position of the ownship [d,d,d]
29  * vo : Ownship velocity vector [d/t,d/t,d/t]
30  * vi : Traffic velocity vector [d/t,d/t,d/t]
31  *
32  * Functions
33  * ---------
34  * LoS : Check for 3-D loss of separation
35  * detection : 3-D conflict detection with calculation of conflict interval
36  * cd3d : Check for predicted conflict
37  *
38  * Global variables (modified by detection)
39  * ----------------
40  * t_in : Time to loss of separation
